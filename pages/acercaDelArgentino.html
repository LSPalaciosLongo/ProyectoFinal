ABOUT SPEAKING LIKE AN ARGENTINIAN

This is a proyect and a social space that the objective is help to english speakers to learn the spanish language.

First of all, the most important thing about is the complex and extense knowledge. Understand the lenguage as a dynamic phenomenon. There are many dialects, around the world and inside each country, all of these with the same importance. But the question is “How i can start?”… Well, ¡inmerse into each one!

Many european people have the possibility to learn the Rioplatense spanish dialect, spoken in Buenos Aires (and Uruguay), that is a good introduction, but we can’t forget the others. Any way, if someone know the usual social context and cultural meanings, and know how act properly in everyday situation, is easy to communicate with spanish speakers, from México to Argentina, Cuba and Colombia, and from others continents as Spain or Guinea Ecuatorial (Africa). What all of these kind have in common is what gives up the possibility of communicating between al spanish speakers.

So, is very important for us to show you and think with you the many significates of the culture and history of Argentina and America Latina. Study the words and definitions as abstract concepts doesn’t have many importance, but if we learn the cultural meanings, we can act properly and have a serious conversation with native people.

Unlike others teaching pedagogies, we concibe the lenguage as medium of communication. No just signe and significate, otherwise as the capacity to refer in a context and build relations between speakers, with many conditions (social + cultural + politics + pragmatics). In this sence, the spanish lenguage teaching is in search of the intercultural speaker (no the native speaker), the one that, according to their different socialcultural contexts, trigger many differents of interpretations.

This pedagogy of the appropriate (no the authentic) is oriented to understand the cultural and social situations, discuss the authority of the native speaker and the work of the sociopragmatic competence and the freedom to adapt and ashare value judgaments. So, since this moment, we prefer the concept “profer speaker”, instead “native speaker”.
